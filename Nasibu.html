About Me

My name is Nasib Yasin Said and I am a first-year Computer Science student at the University of Dar es Salaam. I was born and raised in Kawe, Dar es Salaam — a city that has shaped my passion for creativity and technology.

I am both a programmer and a creative. I run my own comic studio where I write, design and direct original comic book series. I believe storytelling and technology go hand in hand, and my goal is to one day merge both worlds through game development.

I am working towards becoming a professional game developer and cybersecurity expert, combining technical skill with creative vision.
